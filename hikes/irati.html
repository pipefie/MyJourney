Selva de Irati - Navarra

The Selva de Irati, located in Navarra, is one of the largest and most beautiful beech and fir forests in Europe, stretching over 17,000 hectares. It is a place of tranquility, dense greenery, and diverse wildlife. My girlfriend and I embarked on a circular hiking trail here that was unforgettable. This hike led us through ancient trees and thick undergrowth, eventually crossing the border into France at one point, adding an international flair to our adventure.

The trails in Irati vary in difficulty, but the one we chose was moderately challenging, perfect for those looking for a full day in nature. The paths were well marked, and we even took a moment to appreciate the stunning views over the forested mountains that seemed to stretch endlessly. If you're a nature lover, the Selva de Irati is a must-visit. Do not forget to pack a picnic there are plenty of scenic spots to stop and enjoy the serene atmosphere.
