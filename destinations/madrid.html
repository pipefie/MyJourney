Discovering Madrid

Madrid, the vibrant capital of Spain, is one of those cities where history, culture, and modern vibes come together seamlessly. As a data science student who loves a good balance between history and technology, I found Madrid to be a perfect mix. My girlfriend and I spent hours exploring iconic spots like the Templo de Debod and the bustling Plaza Mayor.

One thing I love about Madrid is how walkable the city is. We could easily stroll from the beautiful Retiro Park to the world-famous Prado Museum. As huge foodies, we couldn not leave without trying traditional dishes like cochinillo at Casa Botín, one of the oldest restaurants in the world, and the also traditional Churros con Chocolate at San Gines.

The nightlife is also amazing! We spent one evening exploring **Malasaña**, a trendy neighborhood full of bars, live music, and a really laid-back atmosphere. It is definitely a city that never sleeps.
