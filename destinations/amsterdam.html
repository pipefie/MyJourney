Adventures in Amsterdam

Amsterdam was a perfect getaway for us. I loved how the city balances its rich history with a super modern vibe. From cruising the canals to wandering around the Van Gogh Museum, there is always something to see. It is also super easy to get around, especially by bike, which my girlfriend and I loved!

Amsterdam's coffee culture is also amazing. We spent some lazy mornings in small cafes, people-watching and sipping "Dutch coffee". For lunch, we could not resist trying a stroopwafel or two. The Ane Frank house was another highlight; the history and importance it carries.

At night, we explored the lively, and a bit infamous, streets of the Red Ligth District, a street full of energy and man looking for something else (iykyk). Amsterdam felt both relaxed and exciting, and I wish to go back and explore more.
